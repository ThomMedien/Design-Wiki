# Image and Sound

The training to become a Media Designer for Image and Sound (Mediengestalter Bild und Ton) in Germany is a dual vocational training program that includes both practical work in a company and theoretical instruction in a vocational school. It prepares trainees to work in the fields of film, television, radio, online media, and event production.

**Core Contents of the Training:**

*   **Image Design:** Camera technology, lighting, image composition, editing techniques, and post-production.
*   **Sound Design:** Microphoning, sound recording, mixing, sound design, and sound editing.
*   **Technical Understanding:** Operation and maintenance of professional equipment for image and sound production.
*   **Dramaturgy and Storytelling:** Development of concepts, screenplays, and editorial content.
*   **Media Law and Economics:** Fundamentals of copyright law, production processes, and project management.
*   **IT Skills:** Handling relevant software for editing, compositing, sound processing, and file management.

Graduates are able to independently or as part of a team realize complex media productions from conception to completion.
